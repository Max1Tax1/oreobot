Valorant related commands
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
`-valorant featured (option)`
Shows the featured bundle with statistics
no options: Shows the bundle description
`list`: Lists the bundle contents + price
`skins`: Shows skins in the bundles
`buddies`: Shows gun buddies in the bundles
`cards`: Shows player cards in the bundles
`sprays`: Shows sprays in the bundles


`-valorant stats [riotID#hastag]`
:warning:UNDER CONSTRUCTION:warning:
Shows a player's statistics
