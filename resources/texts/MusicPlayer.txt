Commands related to using the music player & queue
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
`-play`
Kanna joins channel to play songs in the music queue
Plays song if paused

`-pause`
Pauses the current playing song

`-cp`
Displays the currently playing track

`-loop`
Turn on/off looping mode

`-queue`
Shows information about the music queue

`-remove (optional number)` OR `-remove next`
Remove a song from the queue, "next" removes the next song

`-purge`
Stops music player and clears the queue (with a mighty beam)
