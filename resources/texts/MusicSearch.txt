Commands related to searching and adding music
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
`-play [song name]`
Plays the song specified
Adds it to the queue if one is already playing
Kanna joins channel if not in channel

`-add [song name]`
Adds a song to the queue

`-addurl [URL]`
Adds from the URL (may not work for sites other than youtube!)

`-search [song name]`
Searches for the song name
Enter a number to select from the results

`-addprev`
Adds the previous track to queue
